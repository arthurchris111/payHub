Why Use Virtual Card?

Virtual cards, also known as digital or online cards, are a modern and secure form of payment that exists only in the digital realm. They serve as a virtual representation of traditional payment cards like credit and debit cards. Virtual cards are designed for use in online transactions, and they offer a range of unique features and benefits.
